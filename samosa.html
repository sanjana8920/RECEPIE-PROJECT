Delicious Samosa Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 4 medium potatoes (boiled and mashed)
  • 1 cup peas (boiled)
  • 2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying
  • Water (as needed)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix flour and a pinch of salt. Add water gradually and knead into a smooth dough. Cover and set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat a little oil and add cumin seeds. When they crackle, add the mashed potatoes and peas.
  3. Add garam masala, chili powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for a few minutes. Let the filling cool.
  4. Divide the dough into equal portions and roll each into a ball. Roll out each ball into a thin oval shape.
  5. Cut the oval in half. Take one half, form a cone by folding, and seal the edge with water.
  6. Fill the cone with the potato mixture and seal the open edge with water, pinching tightly.
  7. Heat oil in a deep pan. Fry the samosas on medium heat until golden brown.
  8. Drain on paper towels and serve hot with chutney!
