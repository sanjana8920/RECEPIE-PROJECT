Delicious Burger Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef (80% lean)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 4 burger buns
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Cheese slices (optional)
  • Condiments (ketchup, mustard, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine ground beef, salt, and pepper. Mix gently.
  2. Divide the mixture into 4 equal portions and shape them into patties.
  3. Preheat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until desired doneness.
  5. Add cheese slices on top of the patties during the last minute of cooking, if using.
  6. Toast the burger buns on the grill or skillet until golden brown.
  7. Assemble the burgers by placing a patty on each bun and adding lettuce, tomato, and condiments.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy!
