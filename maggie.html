Quick Maggi Noodles Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pack Maggi noodles
  • 1 3/4 cups water
  • 1 sachet Maggi tastemaker
  • Optional: vegetables (peas, carrots, etc.)
  • Optional: 1 egg
  • Optional: chopped green chilies

Instructions:

  1. Boil 1 3/4 cups of water in a saucepan.
  2. Add the Maggi noodles and cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Add the Maggi tastemaker and stir well.
  4. If using, add chopped vegetables and cook for another minute.
  5. For a richer flavor, you can add a beaten egg and stir until cooked.
  6. Add green chilies if you like it spicy.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
